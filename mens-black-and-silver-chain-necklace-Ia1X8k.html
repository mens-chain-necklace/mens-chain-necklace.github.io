Explaining the Composition and Benefits of Sterling Silver Necklace Mens (925 Silver): Sterling silver is an alloy containing 92.5% pure silver and 7.5% other metals, typically copper. Differentiating Between Solid Sterling Silver and Plated Silver: It's vital to distinguish between solid sterling silver and plated silver. Solid sterling silver means the entire piece is made of the 92.5% silver alloy. In contrast, plated silver involves a thin layer of silver applied over a base metal (like copper or brass). While plated silver chains are more affordable, they are prone to tarnishing, chipping, and revealing the base metal over time.
