What to Avoid: Harsh Cleaners, Abrasives, and Prolonged Water Exposure

While cleaning is vital, certain substances and practices can irreversibly damage your **mens chain necklace**.   • Hanging Storage: For longer **mens chain necklace** pieces, consider a hanging jewelry organizer. This keeps chains straight, preventing kinks and tangles. Just ensure the hooks or pegs are smooth to avoid scratching.
